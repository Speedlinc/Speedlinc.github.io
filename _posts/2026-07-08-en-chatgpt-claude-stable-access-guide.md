## Why ChatGPT and Claude Connections Fail

Two things typically cause the problem:

1. **Regional routing issues** — your traffic takes a long, congested path to reach OpenAI or Anthropic's servers, adding latency and packet loss
2. **Flagged IP addresses** — if you're using a free proxy or a crowded VPN server, the exit IP may already be flagged as a data center or "dirty" IP, triggering rate limits, CAPTCHAs, or outright blocks

Free and low-cost VPNs are especially prone to the second issue, since thousands of users often share the same handful of exit IPs.
